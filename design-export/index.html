🎙 Listening…

  1. i
    Live voice on the counter
    Say "next row", "set row to 100", or ask "what's sl1 k2tog psso" without putting the needles down. Basic commands stay offline.
  2. ii
    Pattern intelligence
    Pattern viewer reads each row aloud for you. Tap any instruction for a plain-language explanation. Paste a gnarly increase sentence and the calculator fills itself.
  3. iii
    Stash and project memory
    Scan a yarn label. Fiber, weight, meters, dye lot filed automatically. Ask for a project summary and the AI picks up where you left off, yarn compatibility and all.
  4. iv
    Voice or text journal
    Mumble a session note into the mic or type a quick line. The AI adds date and row, keeps your own words intact.
